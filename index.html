About Me

I consider myself a student of all sciences. I'm a mechanical engineer, software engineer, and scientist. I did my undergraduate at the College of Wooster where I studied computer science and mathematics. I did my graduate at Washington University in St. Louis where I studied mechanical engineering. Right now what peaks my interest is materials science, defense technology, robotics, and artificial intelligence. I desperately want to improve the lives of everyone through advanced robotics and advancements in energy technology.

Recent Work

I think I'll improve it later using character-level natural language generation with improvements on long short-term memory.
